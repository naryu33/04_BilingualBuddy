In this section, you will study greeting in japanese.

1.おはようございます（Ohayougozamasu）

This greeting is generally used until around 10:00.
Often abbreviated to "Good morning" when not used in formal situations.

2.こんにちは（Konnichiha）

This greeting is generally used from about 10:00 to 17:00.

3.こんばんは（Konbanha）

This greeting is generally used after 17:00.

4.こん（Kon）

A franked 「こんにちは（Konnichiha）」.
Not used much nowadays.

5.こんちゃ（Koncha）

A franked 「こんにちは（Konnichiha）」.

6.よ!（Yo!）

Used to greet friends lightly.
For some reason, I often raise one hand.

7.やほ～（Yaho~）

Used to greet friends lightly.

8.おつかれさま（Otsukaresama）

Used in a variety of situations. It is mainly used to treat others or when leaving the workplace.
